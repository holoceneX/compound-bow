Hunt by Reservation

About the Program

Hunt by Reservation connects hunters with landowners through an online platform. It is a win-win solution for managing healthy wildlife populations and creating public access on private land.

Successful hunters in the Minam unit

How to Reserve

  1. Find a property you want to hunt
  2. Enter your reservation information
  3. Receive email confirmation and go on your hunt
  4. Review your hunt
